Focaccia with caramelized onions, cramberries and brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweat caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

image: Sally Vegas

fresh cramberries recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 13/4 cups (350g) likewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil ( scrape rind off with a paring knife if desire, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
